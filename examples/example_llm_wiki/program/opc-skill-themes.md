# Themes

The hackathon focuses on Super Solo founders and recommends seven OPC scenarios, while also allowing creative topics beyond the recommended list.

# Schema

- `theme`: Skill scenario category.
- `what_to_build`: Suggested agent skill pattern.
- `opc_pain_point`: Solo-founder problem area addressed by the skill.

# Examples

| theme | what_to_build | opc_pain_point |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Content Production Automation | Cross-platform content rewriter, trending-topic analyzer, content calendar auto-planner | Publishing consistently across channels |
| Customer Acquisition and Follow-up | Cold email sequencer, LinkedIn outreach skill, follow-up reminders with CRM updates | Turning leads into conversations |
| Workflow Automation | n8n workflow wrapper agent, multi-step API chaining tool, automated task scheduler | Reducing repeated operational work |
| Customer Service | Smart FAQ agent, after-sales follow-up automation, feedback triage with auto-reply | Handling support without a team |
| Knowledge Management and Research | Research brief generator, meeting notes to action list, cross-document synthesis | Turning scattered information into decisions |
| Finance and Admin | Invoice sorting agent, contract drafting skill, monthly finance digest generator | Keeping back-office tasks current |
| Personal Brand Growth | Social metrics monitor, fan engagement reply skill, growth analyzer | Building public trust and audience |
| Creative Topics | Build anything useful beyond the recommended themes | Open-ended founder leverage |
